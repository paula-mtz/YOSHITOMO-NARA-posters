YOSHITOMO NARA: Posters

buy tickets view gallery

A close look to Yoshitomo Nara’s posters, prints and exhibition graphics.

Yoshitomo Nara: Posters, brings together a selection of posters, prints and exhibition graphics by Japanese artist Yoshitomo Nara. The exhibition focuses on the poster as a direct and accessible format, connecting Nara’s characters, bold expressions and graphic style with music, youth culture and visual design.

The show explores how Nara’s images move beyond the museum wall into everyday visual culture. Posters make his work feel immediate, simple and emotional: a face, a colour, a phrase, a mood.
